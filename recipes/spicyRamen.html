Spicy Ramen

I love making this filling, quick, and lazy meal as a student during my busy periods. I think I've figured out how to do this to my preference so I thought I'd share"

Ingredients

  • Samyang Buldak Hot Chicken Flavour Ramen
  • Milk or cream (must be dairy)
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 American slice of cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the noodles, let it cook for 5 mins, stir periodically.
  3. Drain the water and keep about a spoonful of the water left. Turn off the heat.
  4. Add about 1/4 cup of milk or cream and as much of the sauce as preferred.
  5. Add the cheese slice, stir and completely incorporate the cheese
  6. Add the mozzarella, about a handful, and stir. Add the toppings packet, enjoy!
